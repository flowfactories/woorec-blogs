## The Cold Start Crisis: Manual Rules vs. Niche Complexity

**Context**: For `PrintCraft3D`, scaling to `$75,000` monthly GMV exposed a critical flaw: `High print failure rates leading to increased returns and support costs`.

At this stage, standard WooCommerce rules collapsed. Their `3D printing enthusiasts, hobbyists, small design studios, and educational institutions` demanded hyper-specific compatibility guidance. Without technical resources, the team couldn't:
- Recommend printer-calibrated filaments
- Prevent incompatible resin purchases
- Reduce calibration-related failures

The result? 18% return rates and sub-1.8% conversions on every expensive click.

*Powered by WooRec Rule Engine*

*   **Diversity (Scatter/Shuffle)**: Enforced *max 2 consecutive consumables* to prevent category fatigue.
*   **Hard Insertion**: Reserved Slot 4 for tutorial videos (reducing returns) and Slot 10 for high-margin accessories.
*   **Dynamic Weighting**: Boosted low-stock items and high-margin filaments using real-time inventory data.
